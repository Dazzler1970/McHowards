    Tanya McMillan-Howard

    McHoward's Back-Office is a Family Run Business

    Newly, Established in 2019, by Tanya McMillan-Howard and her Husband Daron.

    They realised that most small businesses haven't access to a range of staff or facilities for Bookkeeping, Payroll Management, Human Resource, and in most case even simple Document Processing.

    After many conversations, it was realised that Tanya's experience in each of these areas, could be beneficial for all small businesses

    Tanya was born and grew up in Adelaide South Australia, after finishing school she gained experience in the catering sector and worked in the retail construction sector for 13 years.

    In 1997 she moved with her family to Northern Ireland. She completed her HNC in Business Administration in 2003 and has since worked in back-office administration in the following sectors. Administration in the Community Care Sector, Administration in the Construction Sector, Marketing in the Corporate Signage Sector, Community Care Co-ordination, Finance Assistant, Payroll Manager, Accounts Supervisor and HR Advisor.

    Following the crash in 2008, the business retracted and Tanya found that she was doing more than the one original job. Her experiences have been gained in an economy which required staff to multi-task, significantly cutting back on staff, with many differing experiences, saving the company many thousands in wages. Tanya completed and achieved a Level 5 BTEC in Payroll Management in 2012. In 2017 she completed her CIPD Level 3 in HR Practice.

    Daron Howard

    Daron, was born in South Eastern, Kent in England and after completing school, went to work in the retail sector. His skills related to customer service, which comes from over 30 years talking to people, looking to understand their concerns. In this time, he's worked in various positions from sales floor to lower management, striving to put the customer first.

    In 2016 Daron felt it was time to make a change in his life, feeling that retail wasn't where his dreams lay, he decided to retrain, he chose to start a course Studying Computing and Network Development. He completed his HNC in 2018, with full distinctions. This taught him that complete commitment is essential for all endeavours

    Your data security is important, added to the requirement of conforming with GDPR Regulations, Daron's roll is to ensure complete data integrity and security, ensuring files are digitally backed-up and 100% safe

    This is his Number 1 goal for your business. Always looking to put your needs first

    Our Promise to you is, we will meet and chat with you and work out exactly how we can help you manage your back office better, tailor making a package to suit your experience and business needs, while working within your budget.

    We will not give you things you don't need or understand. We are here to help and make business life easier for you for as much or as little time as you need.

    If you're working on bookkeeping and payroll or personnel management, you're not focusing on your business. McHoward's BackOffice is here to help you, allowing you to get on with the important job of running your business.
