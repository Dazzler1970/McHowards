    McHoward's Back-Office is a Family Run Business

    Newly, Established in 2019, by Tanya McMillan-Howard and her Husband Daron.

    They realised that most small businesses haven't access to a range of staff or facilities for Bookkeeping, Payroll Management, Human Resource, and in most case even simple Document Processing.

    After many conversations, it was realised that Tanya's vast working experience in each of these areas, would be useful for all small business's

    Tanya has spent over 15 years, through the boom time, of the noughties and the subsequent recession, working within the construction industry, covering these rolls for a large company. Rolls that prior to the recession required 4 or 5 employees at all levels to maintain, this would potentially have cost the company 10's of thousands in wage expenditure, while undertaking these rolls.

    She's help the company to fight through the dark times and move towards the good times to come

    If you're working on bookkeeping and personnel management, you're not focusing on your business. McHoward's BackOffice is here to make your Business Run Smoother and your Working Days Easier

    Tanya and Daron, chief aim is to make your business life easier, by handling your paperwork, so you can manage the importamt things
